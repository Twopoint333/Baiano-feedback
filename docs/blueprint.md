# **App Name**: Baiano Burger Feedback

## Core Features:

- User Identification: Collect user's name and phone number for identification and contact purposes.
- Structured Feedback Collection: Gather structured feedback from users through a survey form with predefined questions and rating scales.
- Data Persistence: Store user data, feedback, and timestamps in a Firestore collection for analysis and reporting.
- Google Review Redirection: Redirect users to the Baiano Burger's Google My Business page to submit a review.
- Incentive Communication: Display clear instructions for users to redeem their reward after completing the survey and Google review.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant yellow (#FFC107) to capture the lively and inviting essence of Baiano Burger.
- Background color: Light yellow (#FFF8E1) for a warm and appetizing feel.
- Accent color: Red-orange (#FF5722) to draw attention to key actions and CTAs, in keeping with the street food vibe.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for clear and legible text.
- Use bold and playful icons to represent different feedback categories.
- Mobile-first, simple, intuitive layout with clear progression and large touch targets.
- Subtle loading animations and progress bar transitions to enhance user experience.
